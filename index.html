LETS RAISE OUR VOICE AGAINST AI

"Empowering humanity , Not replacing it ! Join Us in the fight against A.I driven unemployment.

A message from a developer to all developers

Dear Viewers it's high time we understand that tools like Chat-GPT & DALL-E have made development easier, However if its development continues at this rate,in few years companies would simply run on few prompt engineers replacing millions of developers.

Hence We want to raise a petition to bring a law that refrains companies from replacing humans with AI for their profits.

See the below cards to understand better, if you feel the same please support us and feel free to leave Comments.

WHY
IS THIS A PROBLEM ?

This is a very concerning problem as unemployment is the root cause of many problems and it directly affects our economy.

WHAT
HAPPENS IF UNSOLVED ?

Already many companies have reduced their staff as the work is being achieved with less people using A.I tools, In few years when A.G.I comes most of the remaining jobs will be automized.

WHEN
WILL THIS BE AN DISASTER ?

In few decades,Due to the rapid development , AI would dominate humans in many aspects. (Example DALL-E in 10 years would be so creative that no artist could surpass it)

HOW
TO OVERCOME THIS ?

Automation is not new to humanity however it should be benificial so AI should be sustainable , Simply put bring LAWS that prevent companies from replacing humans with AI for profits.
